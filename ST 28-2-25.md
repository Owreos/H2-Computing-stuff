Increased productivity:

- Furthermore, multiple employees can work on the same document simultaneously, allowing for quicker progress on tasks.
- Additionally, the use of online communication channels helps relay information faster within the team and between departments.
- This increases connectivity, which not only allows employees to obtain clarifications quickly but also helps leaders and managers to keep in touch with their employees and manage them better.
- This would reduce a lot of the time that would have been wasted via traditional communication channels and allow employees to use this time to work on more meaningful work.
